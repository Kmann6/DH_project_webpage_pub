## Spreadsheet CSV file, Displayed Dataset, and Submission Guidelines**
The csv file represents information about historical themes in popular culture items. column heads are product_name, manufacturer, product_type, first_year_sold, historical_type, type_name, packaging_text, and packaging_description.

The categories were chosen to record copyright and production information about the product reported and the histroical narratives. This information allows some categorization and some freeform that can be analyzed for common themes. See definitions.

## Definitions
**product_name**: records the title of the product item.
**manufacturer**: records the company name that held the copyright for the game at first publication if known
**product_type**: records the type of product. Type allowed are boardgame, games, books, collectibles, clubs, and decor. 
**year**: records the earliest year of sales or the copyright date.
**historical_type**: records the connection or link to the historical data or reference in the product or marketing. Type allowed are event, figure, or ideology. Event typing must be a specific event such as a specific war or revolution, figure must be historical in nature and with a recorded mythology or story, and ideology includes temporally ambiguous or fictive movements such as revolutionary, enlightened, colonialism, or settler. 
**type_name**: is a freeform value that records the specific language of the historical_type. For example, one type in the spreadsheet is "figure" and the type name is "annieoakley".
**packaging_text**: is a freeform value and records the text on the packaging exactly as it appears. Case and punctuation will be removed during cleaning. 
**packaging_description**: is a freeform value that describes the colors and images that appear on the packaging with basic descriptive language. This category is intended to retain a general idea of the imagery and is not intended to accurately record exact dimensions or colors. 

## Notice on Methodology for sample products
The product information was gathered from game information pages and image archives availble on boardgamegeek.com. All information was verified by archival or copyright records and comparative images were sonsulted for packaging text and description. 

## Notice on Methodology for data cleaning
A Jupyter Notebook was used to clean the dataset. Data was not substainally altered, but used to standardize case, remove punctuation, and ensure dates are recording correctly as integars.
